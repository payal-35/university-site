About Greenfield

A Legacy of Knowledge, A Future of Innovation

Founded in 1892, Greenfield University stands as one of the world's leading academic institutions, combining rich tradition with cutting-edge research and a truly global outlook.

Our 550-acre campus is home to 28,000 students from 120 nations, 3,200 world-class faculty members, and 15 specialized research institutes driving discoveries that change lives.
